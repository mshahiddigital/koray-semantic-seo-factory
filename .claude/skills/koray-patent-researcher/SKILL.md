# Koray Patent Researcher

## Workflow
1. Confirm the research goal (e.g., entity understanding, passage ranking, freshness, site quality signals).
2. Collect candidate patents/papers (only when the user asks for external research).
3. For each relevant document:
   - Summarize the core mechanism in plain language.
   - Extract implications for topical maps, EAV coverage, internal linking, and content structure.
   - List what not to over-interpret (avoid cargo-culting).
4. Convert insights into a concrete checklist for the current project.

## Output
- **Patent/Document table**: `Title | Link/ID | What it suggests | How to apply | Confidence`
- **Project actions** (P0/P1/P2)
