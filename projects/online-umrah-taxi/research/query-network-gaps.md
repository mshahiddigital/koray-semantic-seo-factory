## GAP CLUSTERS — No competitor is ranking for these

These are confirmed content gaps based on competitor analysis (February 2026):

### Gap Cluster A — Audience segments (HIGH PRIORITY)
No competitor has dedicated pages for:
- `women travelers umrah taxi` — safe transport for solo women, mahram-free pilgrims
- `elderly umrah transport` / `wheelchair umrah taxi` — assistance, accessibility
- `group umrah transport` / `group booking 20+ pilgrims`
- `family umrah taxi with children` / `baby seat umrah taxi`

**Opportunity:** These are underserved audiences with high purchase intent and high anxiety.

### Gap Cluster B — Operational/logistics detail (MEDIUM PRIORITY)
- `jeddah airport meeting point taxi instructions` — where exactly to meet the driver at JED Terminal 1/2
- `flight delay umrah taxi` / `what happens if my flight is delayed`
- `ihram miqat stop umrah taxi` — do taxis stop at Miqat boundary
- `prayer stop umrah taxi` / `driver stops for prayer`
- `umrah taxi booking confirmation` — what you receive after booking (anxiety reduction)

### Gap Cluster C — Seasonal/occasion (MEDIUM PRIORITY)
- `ramadan umrah taxi` / `ramadan transport makkah`
- `hajj taxi service restricted zones` — what transport is available vs restricted
- `last 10 nights ramadan taxi makkah` — extreme peak period

### Gap Cluster D — Trust/Compliance (LOWER but important for E-E-A-T)
- `licensed umrah taxi saudi` / `MOT licensed taxi makkah`
- `umrah taxi insurance` / `are umrah taxis insured saudi`
