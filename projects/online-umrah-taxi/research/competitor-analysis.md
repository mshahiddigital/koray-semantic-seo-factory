## Competitor Content Structure Weaknesses

**Universal weaknesses across all 10 competitors:**

1. **No real pricing tables** — they all say "get a quote" without ranges; users leave to find prices
2. **No vehicle capacity specifics** — "sedan available" is meaningless; users need "4 passengers + 3 suitcases"
3. **No JED airport meeting point instructions** — every pilgrim asks this; no competitor answers it
4. **No flight delay policy stated** — high anxiety topic; none address it with a defined policy
5. **No prayer stop information** — pilgrims want to know if the driver will stop for Zuhr/Asr
6. **Schema markup absent or wrong** — umrahtaxi.com uses AutoRental (wrong type); most have nothing
7. **No passage-rankable H2 sections** — headings are vague ("Our Services") not query-matching ("Jeddah Airport Pickup Instructions")
8. **No source context on any claims** — "fastest route", "professional drivers" with zero evidence
