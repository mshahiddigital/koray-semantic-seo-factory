## How Airport Pickup Works

1. **At booking:** Provide your flight number, arrival terminal (Terminal 1 or Terminal 2 at JED), hotel name, and WhatsApp number.
2. **Before your flight:** Your driver sends a WhatsApp confirmation with their name, vehicle model, and Saudi mobile number — 12 hours before scheduled pickup.
3. **On landing:** Your driver tracks your flight in real time. They stand in the arrivals hall holding a sign with your name.
4. **After baggage collection:** Walk to the meeting point — the main arrivals exit at JED Terminal 1 is opposite Gate 5; Terminal 2 is opposite Gate 4.
5. **Free waiting:** 90 minutes from your scheduled landing time. After that, SAR 30/hour applies.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What is included in the price?**
The fixed price includes fuel, tolls, parking at the airport, driver waiting time (up to 90 minutes after landing), and air conditioning. Child seats are available on request at no extra charge.

**Can women travel alone in your taxis?**
Yes. Solo women pilgrims travel safely in our vehicles. Drivers are professional and uniformed. A female companion in the vehicle can be arranged on request for an additional fee — contact us via WhatsApp for details.

**Do you stop at the Miqat?**
Yes. For pilgrims departing from Jeddah Airport or Madinah, the driver will stop at the appropriate Miqat boundary (Qarn al-Manazil for JED, Abyar Ali/Dhul Hulayfah for Madinah) on request. Please specify this at booking.

**Can you transport elderly or wheelchair users?**
Yes. The Toyota Hiace accommodates folded wheelchairs in the boot. A driver assistant can be arranged for pilgrims who need physical assistance — notify us at booking. Route time estimates remain the same.

**What happens if my flight is delayed?**
We monitor your flight number live. If your delay is under 90 minutes, no additional charge applies. For delays over 90 minutes, we contact you via WhatsApp to confirm the revised pickup time and adjust accordingly. Cancellation due to extreme delay is penalty-free.

**How do I book?**
WhatsApp your route, date, passenger count, vehicle preference, hotel name, and flight number to [+966 XXX XXXX]. Confirmation arrives within 15 minutes (business hours) or 60 minutes overnight. Full payment is collected in SAR cash on arrival or via bank transfer prior to travel.
