Every price below is the total cost for the vehicle, not per person. A family of five pays the SUV rate — not five individual fares.

## What Is Included in Every Price

Every quoted price includes:

- Fuel for the full journey
- All tolls on the route (Haramain Expressway tolls, Route 60 checkpoints)
- Airport parking fees (for airport pickup transfers)
- Driver waiting time up to 90 minutes after landing (airport transfers)
- Air conditioning throughout the journey
- One complimentary prayer stop on intercity routes over 200 km
- 24/7 WhatsApp driver support

---

## What Is Not Included

- **Additional waiting time** beyond 90 minutes at the airport: SAR 30 per hour, billed in 30-minute increments
- **Extra stops** beyond the one complimentary prayer stop: SAR 20 per additional stop
- **Driver assistant** for wheelchair or elderly passengers requiring physical boarding help: confirm at booking (priced on request)
- **Child seats** — available at no charge, but must be requested at booking

## Seasonal Pricing (Ramadan and Hajj)

Demand for Umrah taxi services increases significantly during the following periods:

| Season | Price increase | Advance booking recommended |
| ------ | -------------- | --------------------------- |
| Ramadan (entire month) | +20–25% | At least 2 weeks before travel |
| Last 10 nights of Ramadan | +30–40% | At least 4 weeks before travel |
| Hajj season (DhuʼI-Hijjah 1–13) | +30–35% | At least 6 weeks before travel |

Pilgrims who book during standard periods and travel during Ramadan or Hajj pay the seasonal rate — the higher rate applies to the date of travel, not the date of booking. Book early to secure a vehicle and avoid the highest-demand price bands.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Do the prices include a return journey?**
No. All prices listed are one-way. Book the return separately or ask for a return-journey combined rate when booking — we can often offer a combined discount for round-trip bookings on the same vehicle.

**Are tolls really included?**
Yes. The Haramain Expressway tolls between Jeddah and Makkah and the Route 60 tolls between Makkah and Madinah are included in the quoted price. No extra amount is collected at the toll gate.

**Can I negotiate the price?**
The ranges listed reflect market rates. For multi-vehicle bookings (2+ vehicles on the same route and date) or repeat pilgrim groups, contact us for a package rate.

**Is there a cancellation fee?**
Cancellations made 48+ hours before the scheduled pickup time incur no charge. Cancellations within 48 hours: 50% of the quoted price. No-shows on the day: full price charged. See the [full cancellation policy →](/trust-and-policies/).
