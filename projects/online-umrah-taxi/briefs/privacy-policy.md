# Brief: Privacy Policy — Online Umrah Taxi

**Slug:** `/privacy-policy`
**Schema:** WebPage | BreadcrumbList
**Phase:** P1 — launch-critical (legal compliance + E-E-A-T)
**Primary query:** umrah taxi privacy policy
**Word count target:** 900–1,200

---

## Central Entity

Online Umrah Taxi Privacy Policy — the data handling statement covering what personal information is collected during booking, how it is stored, and how it is protected under Saudi data regulations and GDPR (for UK/EU/international pilgrim customers).

---

## Page Purpose

Legal obligation and trust signal. Required for GDPR compliance (UK, EU, Bangladesh — countries with data protection law applicable to international services). Required for Saudi PDPL (Personal Data Protection Law, 2021). Referenced in footer of all pages.

---

## Required Sections (H2 level)

1. **What Data We Collect** — booking data only; no credit card data (cash/bank transfer payments)
2. **How We Use Your Data** — solely to coordinate your taxi transfer; not for marketing without consent
3. **WhatsApp Booking Data** — Meta's role; what OT stores vs what stays in WhatsApp
4. **Data Sharing** — driver only; no third-party sale
5. **Data Retention** — 12 months; how to request deletion
6. **Your Rights** — access, correction, deletion; who to contact
7. **Cookies** — analytics only; no remarketing pixels
8. **Contact for Data Requests** — link to `/contact-us`

## Koray Writing Rules

- **Rule 1:** Each section opens with what we do, then the legal basis
- **Rule 4:** Specific retention period (12 months), not vague terms
- **Rule 17:** Positive framing — "Your data is used exclusively for..." not "We do not sell..."
- **Rule 24:** No absolute guarantees about security ("we use industry-standard measures" not "your data is 100% secure")
