## Central Entity

Online Umrah Taxi Terms & Conditions — the booking contract governing all taxi transfer, intercity, and ziyarah tour reservations made through Online Umrah Taxi.

---

## Page Purpose

Legal compliance and trust signal. Required by Saudi e-commerce regulations and GDPR for UK/EU-origin users. This page is linked from every money page's footer and from the Contact Us page. It is not a hidden legal dump — it is a transparent statement of the cancellation, refund, and service policy that pilgrims need to read before booking.

## Required Sections (H2 level)

1. **Booking and Confirmation** — how a booking becomes valid; WhatsApp confirmation = contract
2. **Pricing and Payment** — fixed price per vehicle; what's included (tolls, waiting, parking)
3. **Cancellation Policy** — 3-tier table: 48h+ / within 48h / no-show
4. **Flight Delay Policy** — 90-minute free wait; what happens beyond 90 minutes
5. **Luggage Policy** — allowed amounts per vehicle type; Zamzam water rules
6. **Passenger Responsibilities** — inform of passenger count and luggage at booking
7. **Vehicle Substitution** — right to substitute equivalent vehicle if required
8. **Liability** — limits on liability for external factors (road closures, Saudi authority restrictions, Hajj crowd control)
9. **Governing Law** — Kingdom of Saudi Arabia law; disputes resolved in Saudi Arabia
10. **Contact** — link to `/contact-us` for questions

## Koray Writing Rules

- **Rule 1:** State the policy term first, then the implication (not the other way round)
- **Rule 4:** All time windows and fees must be numeric and specific
- **Rule 17:** No negative framing — write "A cancellation made 48 hours or more before pickup incurs no charge" not "You won't be charged if you cancel before 48 hours"
- **Rule 24:** Safe answers — no absolute guarantees about road conditions or journey time
