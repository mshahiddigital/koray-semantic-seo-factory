## Central Entity

Madinah Airport to Madinah City taxi — short 15 km private transfer from Prince Mohammed bin Abdulaziz International Airport (MED) to Madinah hotels in the Al-Anabiya, Central, and Aziziyyah districts adjacent to Masjid an-Nabawi.

---

## Key Differentiators

- This is a short 15 km urban transfer (20–30 minutes off-peak; up to 45 minutes peak)
- Significantly cheaper than other airport routes: SAR 80–220 depending on vehicle
- Masjid an-Nabawi pedestrian zone means driver cannot enter within ~300 m; most Madinah hotels are accessible by vehicle
- Pilgrims arriving at MED typically proceed to Madinah hotels first, then take intercity transfer to Makkah later
- MED airport serves primarily domestic and regional flights; most international pilgrims arrive at JED (Jeddah)
