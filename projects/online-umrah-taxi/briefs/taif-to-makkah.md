## Central Entity

Taif to Makkah private taxi — 75 km intercity transfer from Taif hotels to Makkah hotels via Al-Hada mountain road (Route 15) or Al-Sail Al-Kabir highway, with dramatic mountain descent as a journey feature.

---

## Key Differentiators vs Makkah→Taif page

- **Mountain descent:** Taif sits at 1,800 m elevation; the drive down to Makkah (300 m) via Al-Hada road takes passengers through hairpin mountain roads — a journey feature, not a problem
- **Route choice:** Drivers typically use Al-Sail Al-Kabir highway (faster, flatter, avoids the mountain road) unless passengers request the scenic Al-Hada route; specify at booking
- **Ihram at Taif Miqat:** Pilgrims beginning Umrah from Taif must enter Ihram at Wadi Muhrim (the Miqat for people of Taif area); specify at booking — adds 20 minutes
