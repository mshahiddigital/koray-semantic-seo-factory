## Central Entity

Madinah to Makkah private taxi — 430 km intercity transfer from Madinah hotels to Makkah hotels via Route 60 (Madinah–Makkah Expressway), with Bir Ali Miqat stop option for pilgrims beginning Umrah from Madinah.

---

## Page Purpose

Reverse-direction page for `/makkah-to-madinah-taxi`. It is a distinct page because the pickup logistics differ: Haram proximity restrictions apply at the Madinah pickup (Masjid an-Nabawi pedestrian zone), and the Bir Ali Miqat stop is mandatory for many pilgrims departing from Madinah to perform Umrah. This page must rank for "madinah to makkah taxi" as the reverse-direction transactional query.

---

## Key Differentiators vs Makkah→Madinah page

- **Pickup at Madinah:** Pedestrian zone around Masjid an-Nabawi restricts vehicle access; driver meets at the nearest vehicle access point (Al-Anbiyaa Street or Bab Jibreel approach depending on hotel zone)
- **Bir Ali Miqat (Masjid al-Shajarah / Dhul Hulayfah):** Located approximately 9 km southwest of Madinah city centre on Route 60; pilgrims intending Umrah from Madinah must enter Ihram here; stop adds 30–45 minutes
- **Ihram change time:** Pilgrims need time at the Miqat to change into Ihram garments and perform 2 rakaat; driver waits during this process
- **Arrival in Makkah:** Haram proximity zone applies; driver drops at hotel's nearest vehicle access point in Aziziyyah, Bab Al-Umrah corridor, or Jarwal district depending on hotel location
