## Deployment Principle

Hub pages always deploy before their spoke pages. A spoke page must have at least one published page linking to it before it goes live. This ensures no page launches as an orphan.

**Priority tiers:**

- **P1** — Launch-critical. Must go live in first 2 weeks. Revenue pages and trust anchors.
- **P2** — Core authority. Weeks 3–5. Fills route coverage and segment gaps.
- **P3** — Depth layer. Weeks 6–10. Long-tail routes, ziyarah, outer guides.
- **P4** — Completeness. Weeks 11–13. Seasonal, community, and legal pages.
