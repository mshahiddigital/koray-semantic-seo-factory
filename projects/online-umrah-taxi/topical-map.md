# Topical Map — Online Umrah Taxi

**Version 2.0** | Updated February 2026 | 68 pages total

---

## Central Entity

**Primary entity:** Online Umrah Taxi — private licensed taxi transfer service for Umrah and Hajj pilgrims across Saudi Arabia

**Service geography:** Makkah · Madinah · Jeddah · Taif · Riyadh

**Primary user segments:**

- International Umrah pilgrims (UK, Pakistan, Bangladesh, Indonesia, Malaysia — year-round, peak in Ramadan)
- Hajj pilgrims (seasonal — DhuʼI-Hijjah)
- Women traveling solo or without mahram
- Elderly and mobility-impaired pilgrims
- Families with children
- Large groups and travel agencies (10–50 persons)
- Luxury travelers seeking premium vehicles

**Primary search intents:**

- Transactional: book a taxi for a specific route
- Commercial investigation: compare prices by vehicle and route
- Logistical: understand how pickup/drop-off works
- Trust/risk reduction: are the drivers licensed, what happens if delayed
- Educational: Miqat, Ihram, Ziyarah site locations

## Entity Requirements (apply to every Core page)

Every money page must include:

- **Route entity:** origin airport/city + destination city/hotel district
- **Vehicle entity:** model + passenger count + luggage count
- **Price entity:** SAR range by vehicle type + seasonal qualifier + source date
- **Time entity:** distance (km) + travel time (off-peak + peak condition)
- **Operational entity:** booking channel + confirmation method + waiting policy
- **Trust entity:** MOT permit reference + driver language + support contact
